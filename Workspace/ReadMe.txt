 INSTRUCTIONS
o-------------------------------------o
 - Time: Change Time format between 12-Hour and 24-Hour by changing "Format" to %H, for 24-Hour , or %I, for 12-Hour. 
 - User: Middle Mouse click on "Pilot: Name" to open user folder. All text and Number can be edited in User.ini
 - HDD: Change desired hard drive by changing HDD1, HDD2, HDD3 in Variables.ini. Middle Mouse click on "Resource Collection" to open My Computer. Hover over Drive letter and used space to see remaining free space. Left mouse click on Drive letter and used space to change graph to that drive (defaults on HDD1). Middle click on Drive Letter and used space to open that drive in explorer. 
 - NetworkType: Change the substitute from "ADAPTER1" to desired LAN adapter and "ADPATER2" for desired WIRELESS adapter. ** Adapter names can be found in Device Manager under "Network Adapters" - Is CASE sensitive. **
